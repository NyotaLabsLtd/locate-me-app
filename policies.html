Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Locate Me ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users of our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information submitted through our application and services relating to missing persons, including adults and children.

Information We Collect

Information About Missing Persons: Full name, photographs, age, gender, physical description, last known location, date reported missing, circumstances of disappearance, and contact information of the reporting party.

User Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile information, device information, IP address, and login information.

Technical Information: Device type, browser type, operating system, application usage data, log files, and analytics information.

How We Use Information

  • Publish and distribute missing person reports.
  • Assist in locating missing persons and facilitate communication.
  • Verify submitted information and maintain platform security.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

Children's Information

Our platform may contain information relating to missing children. Information regarding minors is processed solely for the purpose of assisting in locating and identifying missing children. We take reasonable measures to protect information relating to children.

Information Sharing

We may share information with the public when a report is published, with law enforcement agencies, emergency responders, and authorized organizations involved in recovery efforts. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may delete information directly through available account features. If unable to do so, you may submit a deletion request by contacting us at nyotalabs@gmail.com.

Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Locate Me is committed to protecting children and supporting efforts to locate, identify, safeguard, and reunite missing children with their families. The safety and best interests of children are central to our operation.

2. Prohibited Content

Strictly prohibited: Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), exploitation content, grooming, human trafficking-related content, harassment of minors, and false reports involving children. Such content will be removed immediately and reported to authorities.

If a child is believed to be in immediate danger, users should immediately contact local emergency services or law enforcement.
