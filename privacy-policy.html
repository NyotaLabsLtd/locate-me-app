🔍 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

Welcome to Locate Me. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when using the app, including:

  • Identity Data: Full name, username, or similar identifier
  • Contact Data: Phone number, email address, and emergency contact information
  • Profile Data: Your username and password, purchases or orders, interests, preferences, and feedback
  • Location Data: GPS location, last known locations, and geographic information

1.2 Missing Person Information

When reporting a missing person, we collect:

  • Physical Description: Age, gender, height, weight, hair color, eye color, and distinguishing features
  • Clothing Details: Description of what the person was last seen wearing
  • Photographs: Recent photos of the missing person
  • Circumstances: Date, time, and location of disappearance; additional notes and context

1.3 Sighting Information

When reporting a sighting, we collect:

  • Location Data: Where the sighting occurred
  • Time Data: Date and time of the sighting
  • Descriptive Details: What you observed, condition of the person, clothing seen
  • Your Contact Information: Phone number for police follow-up
  • Photographs: Optional photos from the sighting

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you access the app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, features accessed, time spent, and crash logs
  • Log Information: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and access times

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Matching & Notifications: To match missing person reports with sighting reports and notify relevant parties
  • Law Enforcement Coordination: To facilitate communication between reporters and authorized law enforcement agencies
  • App Functionality: To provide, maintain, and improve the app's features and performance
  • Community Alerts: To send push notifications about missing persons in your area (if you opt-in)
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security breaches
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and improve user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Law Enforcement

We share missing person reports and sighting information with:

  • Police stations and authorized law enforcement agencies
  • Search and rescue organizations
  • Other government authorities involved in missing person investigations

3.2 With the Public

To aid in community searches, the following information is visible to all app users:

  • Missing person's name, age, and gender
  • Physical description and photographs
  • Last known location (general area, not exact address)
  • Date of disappearance

3.3 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app:

  • Cloudinary: For secure image storage and delivery
  • Neon Database: For secure data storage and management
  • Analytics Providers: To understand app usage and performance

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes).

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • ✓ Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • ✓ Secure Storage: Databases are configured with strict access controls and encryption
  • ✓ Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data
  • ✓ Regular Audits: We regularly review our data collection, storage, and processing practices
  • ✓ Data Minimization: We collect only the information necessary for the app's functionality

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Active Cases: Missing person reports are retained indefinitely until resolved
  • Resolved Cases: Information may be archived but remains accessible for historical purposes
  • Sighting Reports: Retained for the duration of the investigation and beyond for reference
  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter

6. YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@locateme.app

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Locate Me is designed to help find missing individuals of all ages. We handle data related to minors with extreme care:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Missing children's information is shared only for the purpose of locating them and ensuring their safety
  • We work closely with law enforcement and child protection agencies
  • Sensitive details about minors are restricted and handled with additional security measures

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use the app
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and notifications

You can control cookie settings through your device settings.

10. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
