Introduction

Welcome to Locate Me app ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users of our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information submitted through our application and services relating to missing persons, including adults and children.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information About Missing Persons

Users may submit information relating to a missing person, including:

  • Full name
  • Photographs
  • Age and date of birth
  • Gender
  • Physical description
  • Last known location
  • Date reported missing
  • Circumstances of disappearance
  • Contact information of reporting party
  • Additional identifying information voluntarily provided

User Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Login information

Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Application usage data
  • Log files
  • Analytics information

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish and distribute missing person reports
  • Assist in locating missing persons
  • Facilitate communication between users and relevant parties
  • Verify submitted information
  • Improve our services
  • Maintain platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to requests and inquiries
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse

Children's Information

Our platform may contain information relating to missing children.

Information regarding minors is processed solely for the purpose of assisting in locating and identifying missing children and facilitating recovery efforts.

We take reasonable measures to protect information relating to children and limit access to authorized users and personnel where applicable.

Information Sharing

We may share information:

  • With the public when a missing person report is published
